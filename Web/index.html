Experience

Himalkom Website

The Himalkom Website is a platform developed by the Research and Technology team, designed specifically to serve as a comprehensive hub for all activities related to computer science students. It acts as a centralized resource, housing everything from events and projects to important announcements, ensuring that the digital presence of the computer science community is organized and accessible.

VoteLinker

VoteLinker is an online voting website for IPB created to fulfill the final assignment of the Rekayasa Perangkat Lunak course. The idea for VoteLinker arose after observing the shortcomings of the previous offline voting system where students had to physically attend to cast their votes.

BinaryBoutique

BinaryBoutique is a website built to fulfill the final assignment of the Basis Data course. The idea behind creating this website stemmed from the difficulty people face in finding places to learn programming. For this project, we used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which are the most fundamental languages for building website interfaces, and Django for backend development.

TEDxIPB Landing Page

The TEDxIPB Landing Page was developed for the TEDxIPB 2024 event, serving as a key platform to promote and brand TEDxIPB. It functions as a central hub where users can register as volunteers and purchase tickets for the event, making it an essential part of the event’s online presence.
